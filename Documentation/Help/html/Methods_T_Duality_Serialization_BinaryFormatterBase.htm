WritePopOffset
Writes the end of the most recent "safe zone", usually encapsulating a data set. If any error occurs within a safe zone, it is guaranteed to not affect any other safe zone, although the damaged safe zone itsself might be unusable. In general, a safe zone prevents an error from affecting any of the zones surroundings. Safe zones may also be nested.
