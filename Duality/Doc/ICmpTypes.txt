ICmpUpdatable							Indicates a Component that has per-frame updates
	-->	OnUpdate
ICmpGameObjectListener					Listens to its GameObjects "family" events
	-->	OnGameObjectParentChanged
	-->	OnGameObjectChildAdded
	-->	OnGameObjectChildRemoved
ICmpComponentListener					Listens to its Component sisters / brothers
	-->	OnComponentAdded
	-->	OnComponentRemoved
ICmpInitializable						Wants to be properly initialized and disposed
	-->	OnInit
		-->	AfterSave
		-->	Load
		-->	Activate
		-->	AddedToGameObject
	-->	OnShutdown
		-->	BeforeSave
		-->	Deactivate
		-->	RemovedFromGameObject
ICmpCollisionListener					Watches out for collisions
	-->	OnCollisionBegin
	-->	OnCollisionEnd
	-->	OnCollisionActive
ICmpMouseListener						Interested in the mouse cursors movement
	-->	OnMouseEnter
	-->	OnMouseLeave
	-->	OnMouseOver
	-->	OnMouseDown
	-->	OnMouseUp
	-->	OnMouseDrag
